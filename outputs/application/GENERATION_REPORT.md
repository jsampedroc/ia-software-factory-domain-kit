## 💡 Business Concept
A comprehensive Dental Clinic Management System focused on the Patient Lifecycle. Core features: 1. Patient Identity Management: Detailed profiles with medical alerts, allergies, and digital consent tracking. 2. Electronic Health Records (EHR): Clinical notes, digital odontograms, and treatment history. 3. Appointment Orchestration: Real-time scheduling integrated with dentist availability. 4. Billing & Financials: Automated invoicing for treatments, payment plan management, and insurance integration. 5. Security: Role-based access for Receptionists, Dentists, and Administrators.
